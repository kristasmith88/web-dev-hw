Baked Apples

Fall has officially arrived! This means its time for pumpkin patches, Halloween costumes, and of course... baked apples! I grew up with baked apples being a staple to celebrate the brisk air and changing of leaves, and in celberation Im making my favorite receipe of this delicious desert!

Here's what we'll need:

  • 4 Honeycrips Apples large
  • 2 tbls Maple Syrup
  • 4 tbls Butter softened
  • 1/4 cup Oatmeal
  • 3/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Pecans chopped
  • Walnuts
  • Additional Tools
    1. Wooden Spoon
    2. Baking Dish
    3. Large Bowl
    4. measuring cups
    5. Knife
    6. Spoon
