Instructions

Instructions First
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
 Second
Wash honeycrisp apples and core out center leaving enough room for filling.
 Third
Combine butter, pecans, brown sugar, and cinnamon in bowl.
Fourth
Fill centers of each apple to the brim of the core. Lightly drizzle with Maple Syrup.
 Fifth
Fill 2quart baking dish with 3/4 cups of water. Place apples upright in dish and bake for 1 hour.
For even better results, top each apple with scoop of vanilla ice cream before serving!
